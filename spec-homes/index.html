Why Otto Specs

What makes our specs different.

01

Designed, not templated

Every spec home gets a unique floor plan designed for its lot. We don't pull from a catalog. We design from scratch, same as our custom work.

02

Same materials, same hands

The subs, the suppliers, the standards. Identical to our custom builds. If we wouldn't use it in a client's home, it doesn't go in ours.

03

We stake our name on it

Our specs carry the Otto name just like our customs. There's no B-team, no cost-cutting behind closed doors. Our reputation rides on every home.

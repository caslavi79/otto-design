Design + Build

What custom really means.

A custom home isn't just picking finishes from a catalog. It's building something that doesn't exist yet, designed around how you actually live, on the lot you choose, with every detail figured out before the first shovel hits dirt.

Most builders separate design from construction. That means your vision gets passed through multiple teams who weren't in the room when you described it. At Otto, we handle design, engineering, permitting, and construction in-house. The people drawing your plans are the people building your home.

Why Otto

What makes us different.

01

Design-build, for real

Not "we'll work with your architect." We design it, we engineer it, we build it, and we stand behind all of it. One contract, one point of accountability. That's how we build every home, and it's how we'll build yours.

02

You're talking to the owners

Andrew and Peter. That's who you call, who shows up, who makes decisions on site. We don't have a sales team. We don't have a "project coordinator" buffering you from the builder. When you call, a Kveton answers.

03

We don't disappear behind drywall

The work that matters most is the work you'll never see: framing, electrical routing, insulation, drainage. We sweat what's behind the walls because that's what makes a house last. And we document everything so you can see it too.

Design + Build

One team. Your vision. No translation errors.

Most custom home experiences involve handing off between an architect, a designer, and a general contractor, hoping they're all on the same page. They usually aren't.

At Otto, we do it all. Design, engineering, permitting, construction. All managed by the same two people from start to finish. That means your vision doesn't get diluted, your budget doesn't get inflated by miscommunication, and someone who actually cares about the outcome is making decisions on your behalf every single day.

Why Otto

What makes us different.

01

Design-build, for real

Not "we'll work with your architect." We design it, we build it, we stand behind all of it. One contract, one point of accountability, zero finger-pointing. The people sketching your floor plan are the same people pouring your foundation.

02

You're talking to the owners

Andrew and Peter. That's who you call, who shows up, who makes decisions on site. We don't have a sales team. We don't have a "project coordinator" buffering you from the builder. When you call, a Kveton answers.

03

We don't disappear behind drywall

The work that matters most is the work you'll never see: framing, electrical routing, insulation, drainage. We obsess over what's behind the walls because we know it's what makes a house last. And we document everything so you can see it too.
